Our Work

Home Energy Audit – What We Do:

  • analyze utility bills
  • scan walls for air infiltration, missing insulation, and other hidden problems using thermographic imaging
  • depressurize the home using blower door technology to determine air leaks
  • check for health and safety issues

Home Energy Assessment includes:

  • List of areas in your home where energy is being wasted
  • Thermographic images that detect what the human eye can’t see
  • Suggestions to improve the energy efficiency of your home
  • Estimated cost of improvements
  • Projections of annual savings on utility bills after improvements are implemented.
